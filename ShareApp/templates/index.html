With the increase of environmental concerns and the congestion of roads, carpooling has gained a lot of popularity when it comes to environment-friendly and cheap ways of travelling. Carpooling is when two or more persons share a ride in one of their personal cars. Carpooling reduces pollution since

we have less cars on the road. It’s also economic since the travel expenses are shared among the riders. Travelling alone may be stressful, so having other persons with you on a trip reduces the stress and is also the occasion to socialize and make the trip funnier.
